# Code Of Conduct

Manuscript Lab is for careful, durable writing work. Contributions should keep
that same spirit: specific, respectful, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and direct.
- Assume good intent, but name concrete risks.
- Keep critique focused on code, docs, prompts, workflows, and outcomes.
- Respect private writing material and confidential source text.
- Avoid sharing someone else's manuscript, model-call logs, or private project
  artifacts without explicit permission.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing secrets, private manuscripts, private review logs, or confidential
  source material.
- Repeatedly derailing technical or editorial discussion after maintainers ask
  you to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, close, or remove contributions that violate these
expectations. Serious or repeated violations may result in being blocked from
the project.
